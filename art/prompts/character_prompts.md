## Prompt Composition Rules

1. **Character must fill 60%+ of frame** — never small or distant
2. **Candlestick charts always in background** — massive scale
3. **Low camera angle** — makes characters feel powerful
4. **Cyberpunk lighting** — rim lights, neon, volumetric
5. **Particle effects** — data particles, energy, element-specific
6. **Aspect ratios**: Base 3:4, Epic 16:9, Closeup 1:1
7. **Resolution**: Always specify 8k for maximum detail
