# CoinBattle Saki — AI Research Lab

## Current Research Priorities

### 1. Strategy Balance
- Momentum vs Mean Reversion win rates
- HFT edge in low-volatility environments
- Parameter sensitivity analysis for entry/exit thresholds

### 2. Visual Impact
- Character scale relative to viewport
- Particle density at each stage
- Color contrast ratios for readability during battle

### 3. Market Simulation Fidelity
- Compare random walk statistics to actual BTC/JPY
- Volatility clustering accuracy
- Spike frequency calibration

### 4. Player Engagement
- Battle duration sweet spot (currently 30s)
- Stage escalation pacing
- Reward feedback timing

## Open Questions
- Should characters have adaptive strategies that evolve?
- How to balance spectacle vs readability?
- What metrics best predict player engagement?
