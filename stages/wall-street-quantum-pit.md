# Stage 02: Wall Street Quantum Pit ウォール街量子ピット

## Location
New York, USA — Zone 2

## Visual Theme
Underground trading pit beneath Wall Street. Massive screens display real-time market data on every surface. The floor is a giant LED displaying order book depth. Traders' ghosts (holographic remnants of the human trading era) flicker in the background. Everything is aggressive, loud, competitive.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Opening bell — 9:30 AM eternal
- **Weather**: Indoor — no weather, but electrical storms from server racks
- **Lighting**: Harsh white fluorescent + red/green ticker accent
- **Music**: Aggressive electronic with stock ticker percussion
- **Crowd**: Holographic Wall Street traders screaming orders

## Arena Layout
```
[Massive Ticker Board — spans entire ceiling]
          |
    [Octagonal Pit — sunken trading floor]
   /    |    \
[NYSE]  |  [NASDAQ]
      [Fed]
```

## Stage Special Effect
**Alpha Rush**: Trading volume is 2x normal. More opportunities, more risk.

## Stage Hazard
**Flash Crash**: 5% chance per battle of a sudden 10% price drop that recovers in 3 seconds. Tests every bot's reaction speed and stop-loss discipline.
