Seoul, South Korea — Zone 7

- **Weather**: Confetti and neon rain
- **Lighting**: Aggressive neon pink, blue, and white strobes
- **Music**: K-pop EDM fusion at maximum volume
- **Crowd**: Screaming fans with holographic lightsticks

**Speed Boost**: All execution times halved. The fastest bots become even faster. Scalpers dominate here.
