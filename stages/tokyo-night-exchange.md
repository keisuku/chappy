# Stage 01: Neo-Kabutocho Digital Exchange 新兜町デジタル取引所

## Location
Tokyo, Japan — Zone 1

## Visual Theme
Neon-lit Japanese financial district at night. Giant holographic candlestick charts projected onto skyscraper walls. Cherry blossom petals made of data pixels drift through the air. Traditional torii gates frame the arena entrance, glowing with electric blue light. Shinkansen data streams race overhead.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Perpetual midnight
- **Weather**: Light digital rain (like cherry blossoms but data)
- **Lighting**: Cool blue and white neon, warm golden lantern accents
- **Music**: Lo-fi electronic with shamisen samples
- **Crowd**: Holographic spectators in traditional + cyberpunk mix

**Precision Bonus**: All bots gain +10% accuracy in this arena. Tokyo rewards clean, disciplined trading.
