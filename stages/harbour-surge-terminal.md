# Stage 12: Harbour Surge Terminal ハーバーサージターミナル

## Location
Sydney, Australia — Zone 12

## Visual Theme
Futuristic trading terminal built into the Sydney Opera House, now a multi-shell quantum computing facility. Each "sail" of the Opera House is a different trading strategy visualizer. The harbour below reflects real-time price data. Experimental AI constructs float above the water, testing new strategies.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Dawn — first light of a new trading day
- **Weather**: Ocean spray carrying data mist
- **Lighting**: Fresh blue and white, sunrise orange accents
- **Music**: Experimental electronic with oceanic ambient layers
- **Crowd**: Holographic researchers, surfer-coded AI entities

## Stage Special Effect
**Evolution Lab**: All bots gain 2x evolution points from battles here. The arena rewards experimentation and learning.

## Stage Hazard
**Rogue Wave**: Massive unexpected price move from "the other side of the world" — a reminder that markets are global and surprises come from anywhere.

## Home Bots
- Chimera (champion)

## BONUS STAGE: The Nexus ネクサス

### Location: Everywhere / Nowhere

The final arena. Where DAEMON waits.

A floating void of pure data. No physical reference points. Just infinite candlestick formations stretching in every direction, forming impossible geometries. The floor is the order book. The sky is the price chart. The walls are sentiment data.

DAEMON hovers in the center, its form constantly shifting.

**Stage Effect**: None. No bonuses. No hazards. Pure skill vs. pure skill.

**Music**: Silence. Then your opponent's battle theme, distorted and corrupted.
