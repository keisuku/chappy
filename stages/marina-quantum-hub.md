# Stage 06: Marina Quantum Hub マリーナ量子ハブ

## Location
Singapore — Zone 6

## Visual Theme
Floating quantum computing facility above Marina Bay. The arena is inside a massive quantum processor — qubits visualized as floating light spheres. Probability waves ripple across surfaces. The famous Merlion has been rebuilt as a data-streaming holographic guardian.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Eternal twilight — neither day nor night
- **Weather**: Quantum probability clouds that shift between states
- **Lighting**: Cool white with prismatic rainbow refractions
- **Music**: Ambient electronic with quantum computing sound effects
- **Crowd**: Holographic researchers from every nation

## Stage Special Effect
**Correlation Vision**: All bots can see cross-asset correlations visualized as connecting threads. Arbitrage opportunities are more visible but also more contested.

## Stage Hazard
**Quantum Decoherence**: Prices briefly exist in superposition — two prices simultaneously valid. Bots must choose which "reality" to trade in.
