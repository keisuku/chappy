# Stage 03: The City Clockwork Exchange 時計仕掛けの取引所

## Location
London, UK — Zone 3

## Visual Theme
Victorian-steampunk trading hall. Massive clockwork mechanisms drive the market — literal gears and cogs turning with each trade. Brass pipes carry data instead of steam. The entire arena is one enormous clock, with the battle happening on the clock face. Big Ben visible through stained glass windows.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Tea time — 4:00 PM eternal
- **Weather**: Perpetual London fog with golden gear particles
- **Lighting**: Warm amber gaslight + copper reflections
- **Music**: Classical orchestral with mechanical percussion
- **Crowd**: Well-dressed holographic gentlemen with monocles and top hats

## Stage Special Effect
**Patience Reward**: Bots that wait longer between trades receive a +15% P&L multiplier. Rewards the event-driven and patient strategies.

## Stage Hazard
**Brexit Echo**: Random regulatory event that changes the rules mid-battle. Spreads widen, liquidity drops, and only the most adaptable survive.

- Sterling (champion)
- Gemini
