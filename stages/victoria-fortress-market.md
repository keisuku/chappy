# Stage 10: Victoria Island Fortress Market ビクトリア島要塞市場

## Location
Lagos, Nigeria — Zone 10

## Visual Theme
A digital fortress built on the Lagos waterfront. Massive protective walls surround the trading arena — each wall represents a risk management principle. Inside, the arena is a lush digital garden where surviving bots are rewarded with compound growth visualizations. Spider web data networks connect everything.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Sunrise — the dawn of a new day
- **Weather**: Warm golden light with digital fireflies
- **Lighting**: Warm amber, green growth accents
- **Music**: Afrobeats electronic fusion with market percussion
- **Crowd**: Young holographic tech entrepreneurs cheering

## Stage Special Effect
**Survival Bonus**: Bots that end the battle with positive P&L (any amount) receive double rewards. The arena rewards survival over aggression.

## Stage Hazard
**Currency Shock**: Random forex event that impacts crypto pricing. Tests multi-currency risk awareness.
