# Stage 09: Dalal Cosmic Exchange ダラル宇宙取引所

## Location
Mumbai, India — Zone 9

## Visual Theme
Ancient temple reimagined as a cosmic trading center. Massive mandalas on the ceiling display market data in sacred geometric patterns. Pillars carved with mathematical formulas. The arena floor is a star map where price levels correspond to constellations. Incense smoke carries data particles.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Cosmic — stars visible despite being indoors
- **Weather**: Warm incense mist with golden sparks
- **Lighting**: Deep purple and gold, mandala projections
- **Music**: Sitar + tabla remixed with electronic ambient
- **Crowd**: Meditating holographic sages who open eyes during critical moments

## Stage Special Effect
**Karma Balance**: Every win is followed by increased difficulty. Every loss is followed by easier conditions. The arena enforces cosmic balance.

## Stage Hazard
**Monsoon Volatility**: Periodic massive vol spikes that mimic monsoon patterns. Predictable in timing, unpredictable in magnitude.

## Home Bots
- Karma (champion)
