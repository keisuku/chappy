# Stage 04: Pudong Neural Market 浦東ニューラルマーケット

## Location
Shanghai, China — Zone 4

## Visual Theme
Futuristic Pudong skyline with buildings made of neural network nodes. Data flows visibly between skyscrapers like rivers of light. The arena floor is a liquid display showing deep learning weight matrices being updated in real-time. Dragon motifs in circuit patterns.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Dawn — golden hour
- **Weather**: Flowing data mist that parts around moving bots
- **Lighting**: Gold and jade green, sunrise gradient
- **Music**: Traditional Chinese instruments remixed with AI-generated harmonics
- **Crowd**: Holographic AI researchers and dragon spirits

## Stage Special Effect
**Regime Flux**: Market regime changes 3x more frequently than normal. Rewards adaptive strategies, punishes rigid ones.

## Stage Hazard
**Great Firewall**: Random communication blackout — bots lose access to 50% of their data feeds for 5 seconds. Tests how well they trade with incomplete information.
