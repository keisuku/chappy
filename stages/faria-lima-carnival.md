# Stage 08: Faria Lima Carnival Arena ファリア・リマ・カーニバルアリーナ

## Location
São Paulo, Brazil — Zone 8

## Visual Theme
Carnival parade meets trading floor. Colorful floats carry giant market displays. Samba dancers are holographic data visualizations. Confetti is made of ticker tape. The arena floor pulses with rhythm — the market literally dances. Tropical plants made of green candlesticks grow from the walls.

## Atmosphere
- **Time**: Carnival night — eternal celebration
- **Weather**: Warm tropical rain with fire particle effects
- **Lighting**: Hot orange, red, yellow — fire and passion
- **Music**: Samba-electronic fusion with market data percussion
- **Crowd**: Dancing holographic spectators, drummers, fire breathers

## Stage Special Effect
**Passion Multiplier**: Momentum trades gain +20% extra P&L. The arena rewards conviction and bold entries.

## Stage Hazard
**Samba Reversal**: At random intervals, the market reverses direction instantly, like a dance move. Tests whether bots can pivot as fast as the rhythm changes.
