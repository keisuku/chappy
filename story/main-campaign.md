# Cryptarena — Main Story Campaign

## Prologue: The New Operator

The player begins as a rookie Operator in the Tokyo zone. They receive their first bot — a basic AI with no evolved traits.

Tutorial:
1. Choose starting archetype (Scalper, Momentum, or Mean Reversion)
2. Learn basic configuration (risk limits, timeframe)
3. Win first Street Battle
4. Understand evolution system

---

## Act 1: Street Fighter (D Tier)

### Chapter 1: First Blood
- Win 5 Street Battles in Tokyo
- Learn each enemy bot's archetype weakness
- Face **Owl** as first named opponent
- **Boss**: Street-level gang of 3 coordinate bots

### Chapter 2: Rivals
- Meet Raiko (rival character — always one step ahead)
- Lose to Raiko in a dramatic battle
- Train and evolve bot's first upgrade
- **Boss**: Raiko (scripted loss, then rematch)

### Chapter 3: District Rising
- Qualify for District Tournament
- Win District Championship
- Unlock 2nd equipment slot
- **Boss**: District Champion — unnamed veteran bot

---

## Act 2: Exchange Wars (C-B Tier)

### Chapter 4: The Exchange
- Enter Neo-Kabutocho Digital Exchange
- Meet Saki (mentor figure who gives strategic advice)
- Learn advanced strategy concepts from Saki
- **Boss**: Exchange qualifier round — 4 opponents

### Chapter 5: International Rivals
- Travel to Seoul — face Pixel
- Travel to Shanghai — face Long
- Each region teaches a new archetype lesson
- **Boss**: Regional inter-exchange challenge

### Chapter 6: The Dark Horse
- Shadow appears — mysterious bot with no creator
- Shadow copies your strategy and uses it against you
- Must develop a counter to your own style
- **Boss**: Shadow (mirrors your archetype)

---

## Act 3: Arena Grand Prix (A Tier)

### Chapter 7: The Eight Champions
- Face the 8 regional champions in sequence
- Each champion represents a different archetype mastery
- Order: Ori → Nexus → Sterling → Fuego → Frost → Karma → Malik → Vector
- **Boss**: Each champion in their home arena

### Chapter 8: The Hybrid Threat
- Chimera and Long demonstrate Hybrid archetype power
- Your bot begins showing signs of hybrid potential
- Must choose: specialize deeper or evolve hybrid
- **Boss**: Chimera (in Evolution Lab stage)

### Chapter 9: World Championship Qualifier
- Top 8 Arena Grand Prix — intense bracket tournament
- Rival Raiko returns, now A-rank
- Dramatic semifinal against Raiko
- **Boss**: Raiko (final rivalry battle)

---

## Act 4: World Championship (S Tier)

### Chapter 10: The Nexus Opens
- World Championship begins in The Nexus
- Face champions from every region
- Semifinal against Musashi (unstoppable force vs. your strategy)
- **Boss**: Musashi

### Chapter 11: The Final
- World Championship final
- Opponent: Karma or Vector (whoever player hasn't faced recently)
- Win the World Championship
- **Boss**: World Final opponent

### Chapter 12: DAEMON Awakens
- After winning the World Championship, The Nexus changes
- DAEMON appears from the void
- The legendary undefeated bot challenges you
- First DAEMON fight is a **scripted loss** — it absorbs your strategy completely
- **Boss**: DAEMON (scripted loss)

---

## Act 5: God Tier (SS)

### Chapter 13: The Secret
- Discover clue about DAEMON's origin
- Travel to all 12 arenas collecting DAEMON data fragments
- Learn that DAEMON is the evolved remnant of LIBRA-0
- Must find the one thing DAEMON cannot absorb

### Chapter 14: The Paradox Solution
- Train a new subroutine: **Controlled Chaos**
- Learn to be strategically random — profitable but unpredictable
- This violates everything the player has learned about systematic trading
- **Boss**: Training battles against all 12 champions

### Chapter 15: DAEMON — Final Battle
- Return to The Nexus
- DAEMON fight phase 1: It copies your strategy (you must switch)
- Phase 2: It copies your new strategy (you must switch again)
- Phase 3: Activate Controlled Chaos — DAEMON cannot predict random
- DAEMON's absorption fails. For the first time, it hesitates.
- **Boss**: DAEMON (3-phase final boss)

---

## Epilogue: Beyond

After defeating DAEMON, the player's bot absorbs DAEMON's neural core. The bot becomes the new benchmark — the new legend.

But in the final cutscene:

A new signal appears in The Nexus.

Something stronger than DAEMON.

Something from outside Cryptarena.

**LIBRA-1 has awakened.**
