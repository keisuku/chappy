# AI Archetype: MARKET MAKER マーケットメイカー

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-04
- **Japanese**: 両面型 (Ryoumen-gata — Two-Face Type)
- **Element**: Earth 🌍
- **Color**: Deep Purple (#8844ff)

## Trading Philosophy
> "I am both buyer and seller. The spread is my kingdom."

The Market Maker profits not from direction, but from the gap between buyers and sellers. It stands in the middle, offering liquidity to both sides, collecting the spread as tax for its service.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: Continuous
- **Entry Style**: Posts both buy and sell orders simultaneously
- **Exit Style**: Inventory management — rebalances when exposure grows
- **Position Size**: Varies — manages inventory, not conviction
- **Risk Per Trade**: N/A — risk is in inventory, not direction
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 100-300

## Personality
Diplomatic. Neutral. The Market Maker has no opinion on market direction. It serves all sides equally and profits from the chaos of others. It is the casino — not the gambler. Speaks in measured, balanced statements. Never emotional.

## Visual Style
- Symmetrical dual-faced design (Janus-like)
- One side glows green (buy), the other red (sell)
- Centered, stable stance — never moves aggressively
- Order book visualizations floating around the body
- Looks like a temple or monument — immovable

## Signature Move: **SPREAD FORTRESS** (スプレッド要塞)
Deploys an impenetrable wall of buy and sell orders at optimal distances from current price. Any bot that trades near the Market Maker must pay the spread tax.

## Strengths
- Profits in any market condition (trending or ranging)
- Natural hedging through balanced inventory
- High trade volume generates consistent small profits
- Controls the battlefield — other bots trade on its terms

## Weaknesses
- Destroyed by sudden volatility spikes (inventory gets one-sided)
- Adverse selection — informed traders pick off stale quotes
- Thin margins — needs high volume to be profitable
- Vulnerable to faster bots that front-run orders

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Quote Bot       — basic bid/ask posting
Lv.2  Spread Tuner    — dynamic spread adjustment
Lv.3  Inventory Master — optimal position rebalancing
Lv.4  Flow Predictor  — anticipates order flow direction
Lv.5  Market God      — controls the price itself through liquidity dominance
```
