# AI Archetype: MOMENTUM モメンタム

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-02
- **Japanese**: 不退転型 (Futaiten-gata — No Retreat Type)
- **Element**: Fire 🔥
- **Color**: Golden (#ffd700)

## Trading Philosophy
> "Once the blade is drawn, there is no retreat. The trend is your master — follow it to glory or death."

The Momentum bot believes that markets move in waves. A strong move begets a stronger move. The crowd is right in the middle, and the trend is your friend until the very end.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: 5-minute to 4-hour
- **Entry Style**: Enters when trend strength exceeds threshold
- **Exit Style**: Never exits voluntarily. Rides until reversal signal.
- **Position Size**: Large, concentrated
- **Risk Per Trade**: 2-5% of capital
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 3-8

## Personality
Stoic. Unyielding. Once committed, the Momentum bot does not waver. It does not check P&L. It does not fear drawdown. It simply follows the trend with absolute conviction. Speaks in declarations, not questions.

## Visual Style
- Heavy armored chassis — samurai or knight aesthetic
- Thick shoulder plates, reinforced legs
- Trailing energy cape that flows in the direction of the trend
- Eyes glow brighter as conviction increases
- Ground cracks beneath its feet when entering a position

## Signature Move: **TREND SLASH** (流刃一閃)
A single devastating strike that channels the entire trend's momentum into one position. The longer the trend has been running, the more powerful the slash.

## Strengths
- Captures the largest moves in the market
- Simple and robust — few parameters to optimize
- Unstoppable when the market is trending
- Psychological dominance — opponents panic against conviction

## Weaknesses
- Devastated by choppy, ranging markets
- Slow to enter — misses the beginning of moves
- Slow to exit — gives back profit at reversals
- Vulnerable to fakeouts and whipsaws

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Trend Follower  — basic moving average crossover
Lv.2  Wave Rider      — multi-timeframe trend confirmation
Lv.3  Iron Will       — drawdown resistance during pullbacks
Lv.4  Blade Master    — precise trend entry timing
Lv.5  Tide Emperor    — predicts trend inception before breakout
```
