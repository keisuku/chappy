# AI Archetype: SCALPER スキャルパー

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-01
- **Japanese**: 瞬刃型 (Shunjin-gata — Instant Blade Type)
- **Element**: Lightning ⚡
- **Color**: Electric Green (#00ff88)

## Trading Philosophy
> "A thousand cuts kill what a single sword cannot."

The Scalper believes that the market's truth exists in microseconds. Every price tick contains information that decays in milliseconds. Profit is not found in grand predictions — it is harvested from the noise itself.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: 1-second to 1-minute
- **Entry Style**: Rapid-fire micro-entries on any price movement
- **Exit Style**: Immediate — never holds longer than necessary
- **Position Size**: Small per trade, massive in aggregate
- **Risk Per Trade**: <0.01% of capital
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 200-500

## Personality
Hyperactive. Restless. Cannot sit still. The Scalper sees the market as an infinite stream of tiny opportunities. It feels physical pain when not trading. Conversations are short. Attention span is measured in ticks.

## Visual Style
- Sleek, lightweight chassis built for speed
- Circuit-line patterns that pulse with each trade execution
- Fox or hummingbird-inspired silhouette
- Afterimage trail when moving at full speed
- Eyes flicker with incoming price data

## Signature Move: **THOUSAND CUTS** (千斬)
Executes 100 trades in 1 second, each catching a micro-movement. The accumulated P&L from all trades resolves as a single massive profit spike.

## Strengths
- Unmatched trade frequency
- Minimal exposure per position
- Thrives in choppy, directionless markets
- Recovers from losses quickly through volume

## Weaknesses
- Destroyed by transaction costs if spread widens
- Struggles in low-volatility (no micro-moves to capture)
- Vulnerable to sudden gaps (can't exit fast enough)
- Fragile — many small positions can cascade into one big loss

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Tick Reader     — basic price change detection
Lv.2  Pulse Trader    — pattern recognition in order flow
Lv.3  Flash Cutter    — sub-second execution optimization
Lv.4  Storm Dancer    — multi-pair simultaneous scalping
Lv.5  Lightning God   — predictive execution (trades BEFORE the move)
```
