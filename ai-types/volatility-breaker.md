# AI Archetype: VOLATILITY BREAKER ボラティリティブレイカー

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-07
- **Japanese**: 嵐断型 (Arashi-dan-gata — Storm Breaker Type)
- **Element**: Ice ❄️
- **Color**: Aurora White (#aaddff)

## Trading Philosophy
> "Chaos is not risk — it is opportunity. When the storm rages, I am calmest."

The Volatility Breaker thrives where others fear. It trades volatility itself — not direction. When the market panics, it sells insurance. When the market sleeps, it buys cheap options on the coming storm.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: Multi-hour to multi-day
- **Entry Style**: Enters based on volatility regime analysis
- **Exit Style**: Exits when volatility mean-reverts
- **Position Size**: Calculated by volatility surface models
- **Risk Per Trade**: 3-5% of capital (high reward potential)
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 3-8

## Personality
Contrarian. Ice-cold. The Volatility Breaker is the calmest bot in the arena when everyone else is panicking. It does not react to price — it reacts to fear. Where others see destruction, it sees a sale.

## Visual Style
- Ice crystal / aurora borealis aesthetic
- Transparent armor showing internal temperature gauges
- Freezing effect around the body — slows nearby particles
- Eyes that glow white-blue during extreme volatility
- Storm energy contained within a crystalline shell

## Signature Move: **STORM HARVEST** (嵐狩り)
During maximum market chaos (S7-S8 stage), the Volatility Breaker deploys a massive volatility short. As the storm subsides, volatility crushes, and the bot harvests enormous profit from the calm that follows.

## Strengths
- Profits from fear and panic — counter-cyclical
- Huge payoffs during market stress events
- Natural hedge against portfolio exposure
- Thrives in the exact conditions that destroy other bots

## Weaknesses
- Losses during calm markets (pays premium while waiting)
- Can be destroyed by sustained volatility that never mean-reverts
- Complex strategy — many parameters to calibrate
- Timing is critical — too early = bleed, too late = missed

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Vol Watcher     — basic implied vs realized vol comparison
Lv.2  Regime Detector — identifies volatility regime shifts
Lv.3  Storm Reader    — predicts vol expansion/compression
Lv.4  Frost Shield    — hedges perfectly during vol spikes
Lv.5  Aurora God      — manipulates perceived volatility through strategic positioning
```
