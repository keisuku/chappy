# AI Archetype: MEAN REVERSION 平均回帰

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-03
- **Japanese**: 静水型 (Seisui-gata — Still Water Type)
- **Element**: Water 💧
- **Color**: Cyan (#00d4ff)

## Trading Philosophy
> "All things return to equilibrium. The calm mind sees the deepest pattern."

The Mean Reversion bot believes that extremes are temporary. Every spike will retrace. Every crash will recover. Price is a rubber band stretched by emotion — and it always snaps back.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: 15-minute to 1-day
- **Entry Style**: Fades extremes — buys dips, sells rips
- **Exit Style**: Takes profit at mean/moving average
- **Position Size**: Moderate, scales into positions
- **Risk Per Trade**: 1-3% of capital
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 8-15

## Personality
Calm. Patient. Analytical. The Mean Reversion bot watches others panic and sees opportunity. It never rushes. It speaks softly but carries absolute mathematical certainty. Where others see chaos, it sees the inevitable return to order.

## Visual Style
- Crystalline armor with flowing holographic data streams
- Symmetrical design reflecting the concept of balance
- Shield or barrier-type defensive structures
- Probability matrices visible around the body
- Serene, almost meditative posture

## Signature Move: **EQUILIBRIUM LOCK** (平衡封印)
Creates a probability field that calculates the exact reversion target. Locks a position with mathematical precision and holds until the market returns to mean. Cannot be interrupted.

## Strengths
- High win rate — mean reversion works most of the time
- Profits in ranging, choppy markets
- Natural risk management (buying low, selling high)
- Compounds steadily over time

## Weaknesses
- Catastrophically wrong during regime changes
- "Catching a falling knife" — buys too early in crashes
- Struggles when trends persist beyond historical norms
- Can be squeezed if mean shifts permanently
