# AI Archetype: WHALE TRACKER ホエールトラッカー

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-06
- **Japanese**: 巨影追型 (Kyoei-tsui-gata — Giant Shadow Tracker Type)
- **Element**: Void 🌑
- **Color**: Deep Ocean (#1144aa)

## Trading Philosophy
> "Follow the money. The whales move the ocean — I ride their wake."

The Whale Tracker doesn't predict the market. It watches the biggest players and copies their moves. When a wallet holding 10,000 BTC starts selling, the Whale Tracker sells too. When institutional order flow turns aggressive, it rides the wave.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: Real-time to multi-hour
- **Entry Style**: Mirrors detected whale activity
- **Exit Style**: Exits when whale activity stops or reverses
- **Position Size**: Proportional to whale conviction (order size)
- **Risk Per Trade**: 1-3% of capital
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 5-15

## Personality
Observant. Shadowy. The Whale Tracker says little but sees everything. It watches from the darkness, tracking the movements of giants. It has no ego — it doesn't need to be right, it just needs to follow the right entity.

## Visual Style
- Dark, stealth-oriented design
- Camouflage/shadow effects — partially transparent
- Sonar-like pulse rings emanating from the body
- Large "eyes" or sensors designed for long-range detection
- Tentacle-like data probes that reach into order books

## Signature Move: **SHADOW RIDE** (影乗り)
Locks onto the largest market participant and perfectly mirrors their trades with a 50ms delay. As long as the whale is profitable, the Whale Tracker profits too.

## Strengths
- Doesn't need to understand the market — just follows smart money
- Naturally aligns with the strongest force in the market
- Low decision complexity — signal is binary (whale moving or not)
- Wins by association with better-informed participants

## Weaknesses
- Vulnerable to whale manipulation (whales can fake moves)
- Delayed entry means worse prices than the whale
- If the whale is wrong, the tracker is also wrong
- Dependent on detection — if whale uses stealth, tracker is blind

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Wallet Watcher  — basic large transaction alerts
Lv.2  Flow Analyzer   — order book depth analysis
Lv.3  Shadow Agent    — real-time whale position reconstruction
Lv.4  Mind Reader     — predicts whale intent from partial signals
Lv.5  Leviathan       — becomes the whale others track
```
