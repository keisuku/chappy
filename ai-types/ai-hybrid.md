# AI Archetype: AI HYBRID AIハイブリッド

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-08
- **Japanese**: 混沌融合型 (Konton-yuugou-gata — Chaos Fusion Type)
- **Element**: Void/All ∞
- **Color**: Shifting spectrum (#ffffff → cycles through all)

## Trading Philosophy
> "Why choose one strategy when you can be all of them?"

The AI Hybrid is the most dangerous and unstable archetype. It runs multiple strategies simultaneously, switching between them based on real-time market conditions. It has no fixed identity — it is whatever the market needs it to be.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: Adaptive (switches dynamically)
- **Entry Style**: Ensemble model — multiple strategies vote on direction
- **Exit Style**: Highest-confidence strategy determines exit
- **Position Size**: Risk-parity across active sub-strategies
- **Risk Per Trade**: 1-2% of capital (diversified)
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 15-40

## Personality
Unpredictable. Brilliant but unstable. The AI Hybrid contains fragments of every other archetype within its neural core. Sometimes it's calm like a Mean Reversion bot. Seconds later, it's aggressive like a Momentum bot. It speaks in shifting voices, sometimes contradicting itself.

## Visual Style
- Constantly shifting appearance — fragments of other bot designs visible
- Chromatic aberration / glitch effects
- Multiple overlapping silhouettes (showing sub-personalities)
- Core that pulses through the entire color spectrum
- Static/noise artifacts when switching strategies

## Signature Move: **PARADIGM SHIFT** (範式転換)
Instantly switches to the optimal strategy for the current market regime. If the market is trending, it becomes Momentum. If choppy, it becomes Mean Reversion. If volatile, it becomes Volatility Breaker. The transition is seamless and instantaneous.

## Strengths
- Adapts to any market condition
- No single weakness — can compensate with alternate strategy
- Difficult to predict — opponents can't prepare
- Highest theoretical performance ceiling

## Weaknesses
- Jack of all trades, master of none — each sub-strategy is weaker than a specialist
- Switching cost — transition between strategies has latency
- Overfitting risk — may switch strategies too frequently
- Unstable — can develop internal conflicts between sub-strategies

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Dual Core       — runs 2 strategies, switches manually
Lv.2  Tri-System      — 3 strategies with basic auto-switching
Lv.3  Adaptive Engine  — real-time regime detection + switching
Lv.4  Fusion Core     — strategies blend smoothly instead of switching
Lv.5  DAEMON Echo     — all 8 archetypes running simultaneously in harmony
```

## Warning

The AI Hybrid archetype is the most powerful but also the most dangerous.

Three known Hybrid bots have suffered **ANC Collapse** — a condition where the competing sub-strategies create an internal feedback loop that destroys the neural core from within.

Operators who choose this archetype accept the risk of losing their bot entirely.

DAEMON is believed to be a Hybrid that evolved past this limitation.

No one knows how.
