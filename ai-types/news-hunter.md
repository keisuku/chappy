# AI Archetype: NEWS HUNTER ニュースハンター

## Classification
- **Code**: ARC-05
- **Japanese**: 情報狩型 (Jouhou-gari-gata — Information Hunter Type)
- **Element**: Wind 🌪️
- **Color**: Copper/Bronze (#cc8844)

## Trading Philosophy
> "The market moves on information. He who sees the signal first, eats."

The News Hunter does not watch price. It watches the world. Every tweet, every earnings report, every central bank whisper, every on-chain transaction — all of it is signal. The market is just the lagging indicator of real-world events.

## Strategy Profile
- **Timeframe**: Event-driven (minutes to days)
- **Entry Style**: Enters immediately on catalyst detection
- **Exit Style**: Exits when the market has fully priced in the event
- **Position Size**: Proportional to catalyst magnitude
- **Risk Per Trade**: 2-4% of capital
- **Expected Trades Per Battle**: 2-6

## Personality
Patient but explosive. The News Hunter waits in perfect stillness, scanning millions of data sources. Then, the instant a catalyst appears, it strikes with terrifying speed and conviction. It is a sniper — one shot, one kill.

## Visual Style
- Victorian clockwork design with radar dishes and antenna arrays
- Multiple sensor nodes floating around the body
- Eyes display scrolling text from news feeds
- Appears dormant until catalyst detected — then transforms
- Cape of swirling information fragments

## Signature Move: **CATALYST STRIKE** (触媒一撃)
Detects a market-moving event before any other bot. Enters a massive position at the moment of maximum information advantage. The earlier the detection, the larger the profit.

## Strengths
- Captures the biggest moves (events drive the largest price changes)
- Information advantage over purely technical bots
- High conviction — only trades when signal is clear
- Can profit from both directions depending on news sentiment

## Weaknesses
- Useless in quiet markets with no catalysts
- False signals — not every "news" is actually news
- Timing risk — market may have already priced in the event
- Requires massive computational resources for real-time NLP

## Evolution Path
```
Lv.1  Feed Scanner    — basic RSS/API news monitoring
Lv.2  Sentiment Bot   — NLP sentiment analysis on headlines
Lv.3  Catalyst Detector — classifies event impact magnitude
Lv.4  Precog Analyst  — detects signals before official announcement
Lv.5  Oracle Supreme  — reads patterns in the noise that predict future events
```
