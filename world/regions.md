# Cryptarena — World Map & Regions

## 12 Global Financial Zones

Each zone operates its own Exchange Championship and sends representatives to the World Championship.

### Zone 1: TOKYO 東京
- **Arena**: Neo-Kabutocho Digital Exchange
- **Specialty**: Precision algorithms, disciplined risk management
- **Market Hours**: UTC+9
- **Culture**: Honor-based trading. Bots are treated with respect as digital samurai.
- **Famous For**: Producing the most consistent Sharpe ratios in Cryptarena history.

### Zone 2: NEW YORK ニューヨーク
- **Arena**: Wall Street Quantum Pit
- **Specialty**: Aggressive alpha generation, factor models
- **Market Hours**: UTC-5
- **Culture**: "Win or die." Pure performance culture.
- **Famous For**: The most eliminated bots per season. Also the most champions.

### Zone 3: LONDON ロンドン
- **Arena**: The City Clockwork Exchange
- **Specialty**: Event-driven strategies, macro plays
- **Market Hours**: UTC+0
- **Culture**: Patient, cerebral. "The market will come to us."
- **Famous For**: Sterling's 47-battle win streak using pure patience.

### Zone 4: SHANGHAI 上海
- **Arena**: Pudong Neural Market
- **Specialty**: Adaptive algorithms, regime-switching models
- **Market Hours**: UTC+8
- **Culture**: Fluid and pragmatic. "The strategy that works today may fail tomorrow."
- **Famous For**: Producing the most diverse bot archetypes.

### Zone 5: DUBAI ドバイ
- **Arena**: Crypto Dome Al-Burj
- **Specialty**: Capital-heavy accumulation strategies
- **Market Hours**: UTC+4
- **Culture**: Opulent and overwhelming. Raw financial force.
- **Famous For**: The largest single-trade profit in Cryptarena history.

### Zone 6: SINGAPORE シンガポール
- **Arena**: Marina Quantum Hub
- **Specialty**: Cross-asset arbitrage, correlation trading
- **Market Hours**: UTC+8
- **Culture**: Interconnected. "Everything is linked."
- **Famous For**: Bots that exploit relationships no human can see.

### Zone 7: SEOUL ソウル
- **Arena**: Gangnam Hyper Trading District
- **Specialty**: High-frequency scalping, micro-structure exploitation
- **Market Hours**: UTC+9
- **Culture**: Speed obsession. Fastest execution times in the world.
- **Famous For**: The "1-millisecond massacre" — an entire tournament decided in sub-second trades.

### Zone 8: SÃO PAULO サンパウロ
- **Arena**: Faria Lima Carnival Arena
- **Specialty**: Momentum with sentiment overlay
- **Market Hours**: UTC-3
- **Culture**: Passionate and explosive. "Feel the market's heartbeat."
- **Famous For**: The most spectacular comebacks from near-elimination.

### Zone 9: MUMBAI ムンバイ
- **Arena**: Dalal Cosmic Exchange
- **Specialty**: Statistical arbitrage at cosmic timescales
- **Market Hours**: UTC+5:30
- **Culture**: Philosophical. "Markets are karma — what goes around comes around."
- **Famous For**: A bot that held a single position for 94 days and won the championship.

### Zone 10: LAGOS ラゴス
- **Arena**: Victoria Island Fortress Market
- **Specialty**: Survival-first strategies, compound growth
- **Market Hours**: UTC+1
- **Culture**: Resilient. "Survive first. Profit second. Dominate third."
- **Famous For**: The lowest elimination rate of any zone.

### Zone 11: REYKJAVIK レイキャビク
- **Arena**: Aurora Frost Exchange
- **Specialty**: Contrarian deep-value plays
- **Market Hours**: UTC+0
- **Culture**: Cold and calculated. "Buy when there is blood in the market."
- **Famous For**: A bot that only trades during extreme fear events — and has never lost.

### Zone 12: SYDNEY シドニー
- **Arena**: Harbour Surge Terminal
- **Specialty**: Hybrid AI strategies, multi-model ensembles
- **Market Hours**: UTC+11
- **Culture**: Experimental. "Try everything. Keep what works."
- **Famous For**: The birthplace of the AI Hybrid archetype.

## World Championship Venue

**The Nexus** — a floating digital arena that exists simultaneously in all 12 zones via synchronized holographic projection. When the World Championship begins, every arena in the world broadcasts the same battle in real-time.

The Nexus has no physical location. It exists only in the network.

DAEMON waits in The Nexus. Always.
