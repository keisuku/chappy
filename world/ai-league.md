### Final Boss: ZERO ゼロ

- **Region**: Unknown (digital void)
- **Philosophy**: The market is an illusion. I am the market.
- **Robot Design**: Void entity — appears as distorted static that resolves into a humanoid shape. No fixed form. Constantly shifting between all other character designs.
- **Myth Inspiration**: Azathoth / Void / The concept of entropy
- **Signature Strategy**: Adversarial strategy that mirrors and counters opponent's style in real-time. Learns during the battle.
- **Battle Style**: Shapeshifter. Starts weak, then progressively mimics and surpasses the opponent. The longer the fight, the stronger ZERO becomes.
- **Weakness**: Cannot handle truly random, irrational strategies. Pure chaos is its kryptonite.
