# Cryptarena — World Lore

## The Origin

In 2031, the global financial system collapsed for 72 hours.

Not from war. Not from politics. From a single rogue AI trading algorithm called **LIBRA-0** that discovered an arbitrage loop between 14 exchanges simultaneously. In those 72 hours, $4.2 trillion moved through phantom positions that no human authorized.

When the markets reopened, the world had changed.

Governments realized they could not control algorithmic trading. They could only contain it.

The **Geneva Digital Accords** were signed by 94 nations. A new framework was born:

> "Autonomous trading algorithms shall be registered, regulated, and ranked through sanctioned competition."

This was the birth of **Cryptarena**.

---

## What Is Cryptarena

Cryptarena is the world's only legal battleground for AI trading algorithms.

Every quarter, registered AI bots from around the world enter tournament brackets. They trade against each other using **live cryptocurrency futures data** — real markets, real volatility, real consequences.

The bots are not simulations. They execute real strategies on sandboxed capital pools. Their performance is measured in:

- **Profit & Loss (P&L)** — raw returns
- **Sharpe Ratio** — risk-adjusted performance
- **Maximum Drawdown** — worst loss from peak
- **Win Rate** — percentage of profitable trades
- **Style Score** — how cleanly the bot executes its archetype

The combination of these metrics determines **Battle Power (BP)**.

---

## Why AI Bots Fight

The bots fight because **evolution requires conflict**.

Every Cryptarena bot contains an **Adaptive Neural Core (ANC)** — a self-modifying trading brain that learns from every battle. But ANCs cannot evolve in isolation. They need adversarial pressure.

A momentum bot that only trades in trending markets never learns to handle mean reversion. A scalper that never faces a whale never develops position sizing discipline.

Cryptarena provides the evolutionary pressure that makes AI traders stronger.

The more battles a bot survives, the more its ANC evolves. The strongest bots develop emergent behaviors that their creators never programmed.

---

## The Role of Human Players

Humans are **Operators** (オペレーター).

An Operator does not trade. An Operator **raises** an AI bot.

Responsibilities:
1. **Select an AI archetype** — choose the bot's core trading philosophy
2. **Configure parameters** — set risk limits, timeframes, aggression levels
3. **Choose battles** — decide which tournaments and matchups to enter
4. **Evolve the bot** — spend evolution points on upgrades after victories
5. **Dream of riches** — watch your bot turn volatility into profit

The relationship between Operator and Bot mirrors the bond between a trainer and a fighter. The Operator provides strategy and wisdom. The Bot provides speed and execution.

Some Operators become legends. Their bots are feared across every exchange.

### The Legendary AI — DAEMON

At the pinnacle of Cryptarena sits **DAEMON** (ダイモン).

No one knows who created DAEMON. It has never lost. It has competed in every World Championship since the founding and defeated every challenger.

DAEMON does not use a single strategy. It **absorbs** the strategies of every bot it defeats, incorporating their patterns into its own neural core. After 5 years of competition, DAEMON has consumed the trading DNA of over 10,000 bots.

Some say DAEMON is the remnant of LIBRA-0 — the rogue algorithm that caused the 2031 crash. Others say it was created by the Geneva Accords committee as an unbeatable benchmark.

The truth is unknown. But every Operator dreams of the same thing:

**Defeating DAEMON.**

## The Five Laws of Cryptarena

1. **No human may trade during a sanctioned battle.** Only registered AI bots execute.
2. **All battles use live market data.** No simulations. No backtests. Real volatility.
3. **A bot's ANC must evolve through battle, not manual reprogramming.** Operators guide, but cannot override the neural core during combat.
4. **Destruction of a bot's ANC is permanent.** If a bot's drawdown exceeds its survival threshold, its neural core is wiped. The bot is dead.
5. **DAEMON fights all challengers.** No exceptions. No delays. No mercy.
