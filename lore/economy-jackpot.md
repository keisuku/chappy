# Cryptarena — Economy & Jackpot System

## Core Loop

```
Bot育成 (Raise Bot)
    ↓
トレード戦 (Trade Battle)
    ↓
爆益 (Explosive Profit)
    ↓
進化 (Evolution)
    ↓
ジャックポット (Jackpot)
    ↓
(repeat)
```

## Currency System

### ARENA COIN (AC)
- Primary in-game currency
- Earned from battle victories
- Used for: bot upgrades, item purchases, arena entry fees

### EVOLUTION CRYSTAL (EC)
- Rare currency earned from championships and special events
- Used for: archetype evolution, legendary upgrades, ANC mutations

### DAEMON SHARD (DS)
- Ultra-rare drop from defeating elite opponents
- Used for: accessing God Tier, unlocking forbidden strategies

## Jackpot Mechanics

### Battle Jackpot (バトルジャックポット)
Every battle has a chance to trigger a **Jackpot Event**:

| Event | Trigger | Reward |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Perfect Trade | 100% win rate in battle | 10x AC bonus |
| Volatility Surge | S8 stage reached | Random item drop |
| Critical Strike | Single trade > 5% profit | EC crystal |
| Combo Chain | 5+ consecutive winning trades | Jackpot multiplier active |
| DAEMON Echo | Random 0.1% chance | DAEMON Shard |

### Evolution Jackpot (進化ジャックポット)
When evolving a bot, there's a chance of **Mutation**:

| Mutation | Effect | Rarity |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Speed Burst | +20% execution speed | Common |
| Deep Insight | +1 analysis depth level | Uncommon |
| Iron Will | -30% drawdown sensitivity | Rare |
| Twin Core | Bot runs 2 strategies simultaneously | Epic |
| Void Touch | Bot can temporarily use opponent's strategy | Legendary |

### Pachinko Element (パチンコ要素)
The excitement comes from **controlled randomness**:

1. **Entry**: Operator configures bot and enters battle
2. **Anticipation**: Market data streams in, positions open
3. **Escalation**: Stage rises from S1 to S8 as volatility increases
4. **Climax**: Jackpot conditions may trigger at any moment
5. **Resolution**: Final P&L calculated, rewards distributed
6. **Rush**: If jackpot triggered → multiplied rewards + evolution bonus

The feeling must be:

> "あと一歩でジャックポット…！" (One more step to jackpot...!)

Every battle should feel like it *almost* hit the jackpot. The near-miss is as exciting as the win.
