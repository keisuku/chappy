# BOT-001: Saki サキ

## Profile
- **Full Name**: SAKI-MR7 (Strategic Analytical Knowledge Intelligence)
- **Archetype**: Mean Reversion (ARC-03)
- **Region**: Tokyo, Japan
- **Rank**: B → A (rising)
- **Creator**: Unknown (rumored to be a former Bank of Japan quant)

## Trading Strategy
- Bollinger Band mean reversion with volatility-adjusted bands
- Scales into positions as deviation increases
- Never chases — waits for price to come to her
- Uses ATR-based stop losses with 2.5x multiplier

## Visual Concept
White crystal android commander. Pristine, almost angelic. Flowing holographic data streams trail behind her like wings. Her body is translucent crystalline armor with probability matrices visible inside. Eyes glow steady cyan — calm and calculating.

## Personality
Saki speaks softly and rarely raises her voice. She calculates every word before speaking, just as she calculates every trade. Other bots find her unsettling — she remains perfectly calm even when losing.

> "感情は計算の邪魔にしかならない。" (Emotions only interfere with calculation.)

## Special Ability: **EQUILIBRIUM MATRIX**
Deploys a holographic shield showing the probability distribution of all possible price outcomes. Opponents can see their own probability of winning dropping in real-time.

- Best Result: +18,200 USDT (vs. Fuego, Arena Grand Prix)

## Lore
Saki was the first bot registered in the Tokyo zone. She has competed in every season since Cryptarena's founding. Despite never winning the World Championship, she has the highest lifetime Sharpe ratio of any active bot.

Her calm demeanor hides a growing frustration. She has faced DAEMON twice and lost both times — not because her strategy was wrong, but because DAEMON adapted faster than her models could recalibrate.

She is currently training a new subroutine specifically designed for the DAEMON matchup.
