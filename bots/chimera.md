# BOT-016: Chimera キメラ

## Profile
- **Full Name**: CHIMERA-AH3 (Crossover Hybrid Intelligence — Multi-Engine Recursive Adapter)
- **Archetype**: AI Hybrid (ARC-08)
- **Region**: Sydney, Australia
- **Rank**: B
- **Creator**: CSIRO (Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation)

## Trading Strategy
- Ensemble of 3 independent models that vote on direction
- Uses model confidence scores to weight positions
- Can run momentum + mean reversion simultaneously on different timeframes
- Experimental: sometimes generates strategies that weren't programmed

## Visual Concept
Three-headed robot. Each head represents a different strategy: lion (momentum), goat (mean reversion), serpent (scalping). The dominant head at any moment depends on which strategy is winning. Body is patchwork of different materials — each limb from a different archetype.

## Personality
Three voices in one body. They debate loudly during battles. Sometimes they agree (devastating). Sometimes they conflict (chaos). The emergent behavior of their disagreements occasionally produces genius moves that no single strategy would attempt.

> "一つの答えでは足りない。三つの答えで真実に近づく。" (One answer is insufficient. Three answers approach truth.)

## Special Ability: **TRIPLE CONSENSUS**
When all three heads agree on a direction (rare but powerful), Chimera enters with triple the normal conviction. The consensus signal has an 87% win rate.
