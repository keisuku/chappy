# BOT-011: Ori オリ

## Profile
- **Full Name**: ORI-RM7 (Optimal Risk Intelligence)
- **Archetype**: Whale Tracker (ARC-06) / Fortress variant
- **Region**: Lagos, Nigeria
- **Rank**: A
- **Creator**: Self-taught Nigerian programmer, age 19

## Trading Strategy
- Ultra-conservative risk management
- Never risks more than 0.5% of capital per trade
- Follows whale wallet movements with 95% accuracy
- Compounds tiny gains over long periods — the turtle strategy

## Visual Concept
Obsidian fortress android with layered protective shields. Multiple hexagonal barrier layers surround the body. Compact, heavy design — built to survive, not to attack. Spider-web sensor arrays detect threats from all directions. Glows with a calm green inner light.

## Personality
Patient, resilient, wise beyond its creation date. Ori does not seek glory — it seeks survival. It has watched hundreds of aggressive bots destroy themselves. It simply... endures.

> "生き残る者が勝つ。派手に散るのは愚かな者だ。" (The survivor wins. Those who burn bright are fools.)

## Special Ability: **COMPOUND FORTRESS**
Deploys layered shields that absorb drawdown. Each shield represents a risk management rule. To destroy Ori, opponents must break through every layer — and Ori is constantly rebuilding them.

Ori was created by Adewale Okonkwo, a 19-year-old self-taught programmer from Lagos. Adewale grew up watching his community survive economic crises. He coded Ori with one principle: survival first, profit second.

Ori has never been eliminated. Not once in 4 seasons. It is the longest-surviving active bot in Cryptarena history.
