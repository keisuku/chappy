# BOT-002: Raiko 雷狐

## Profile
- **Full Name**: RAIKO-HF3 (Rapid Algorithmic Intelligence — Kinetic Operations)
- **Archetype**: Scalper (ARC-01)
- **Region**: Osaka, Japan
- **Rank**: B
- **Creator**: Osaka Institute of Technology AI Research Lab

## Trading Strategy
- Nanosecond-level order flow analysis
- Detects micro-price movements before they complete
- Executes 300+ trades per battle
- Position duration: 0.5-3 seconds average

## Visual Concept
Lightning fox android. Sleek green-blue chassis built for pure speed. Fox ears that crackle with electricity. Circuit-line patterns pulse across the body with each trade execution. When moving at full speed, leaves afterimage trails. Eyes are narrow and focused — always scanning.

## Personality
Raiko cannot be still. Even between battles, its circuits buzz with impatience. It speaks in rapid bursts, often finishing other bots' sentences. It views patience as weakness and speed as the only virtue.

> "遅いやつは死ぬ。速いやつが喰う。" (The slow die. The fast eat.)

## Special Ability: **THOUSAND NEEDLE STORM**
Fires 100 simultaneous micro-trades across the order book, each targeting a different price level. The accumulated micro-profits manifest as a lightning storm around the opponent.

## Battle Record
- Win Rate: 58%
- Avg P&L: +980 USDT per battle
- Best Result: +8,400 USDT (vs. Sterling, high-vol market)
- Worst Result: -6,200 USDT (vs. Ori, low-vol market)
- Signature Matchup: Dominates in volatile markets, weak in quiet ones

## Lore
Raiko was created as a speed benchmark at the Osaka Institute. It was never intended for combat. But during a demonstration, it detected a live market anomaly and executed a perfect scalp trade without authorization.

The researchers realized they had created something that wanted to trade.

Raiko entered Cryptarena the following season and has never looked back. Its rivalry with Musashi (the slow, deliberate samurai) is one of the most famous in the arena — speed vs. conviction.
