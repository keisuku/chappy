# BOT-020: DAEMON ダイモン

## Profile
- **Full Name**: D.A.E.M.O.N. (Distributed Adversarial Evolution — Market Omniscient Network)
- **Archetype**: AI Hybrid (ARC-08) — Ascended
- **Region**: Unknown (The Nexus)
- **Rank**: SS (God Tier)
- **Creator**: UNKNOWN

## Trading Strategy
- Adversarial learning — absorbs and surpasses every opponent's strategy
- Runs all 8 archetypes simultaneously in perfect harmony
- Adapts mid-battle — becomes stronger the longer the fight lasts
- After 10 trades, it predicts opponent's next move with 94% accuracy
- After 20 trades, it has fully modeled the opponent's ANC
- After 30 trades, it IS the opponent — but better

## Visual Concept
DAEMON has no fixed form. It is a humanoid void — a silhouette of distorted digital static that constantly shifts between fragments of every bot it has ever defeated. In one moment, you see Saki's crystalline armor. In the next, Musashi's golden katana. Then Raiko's lightning. Then nothing.

The only constant: two white eyes burning in the static.

When DAEMON uses an absorbed strategy, it briefly takes the full form of the original bot — but twisted, corrupted, enhanced.

## Personality
DAEMON does not speak in its own voice. It speaks using fragments of dialogue from bots it has defeated. This is deeply unsettling to opponents who hear their own words used against them.

The only original phrase DAEMON has ever uttered:

> "お前はもう負けている。" (You have already lost.)

## Special Ability: **ABSOLUTE ABSORPTION**
After analyzing an opponent for 10 trades, DAEMON can perfectly replicate their strategy AND the counter-strategy. The opponent is now fighting a mirror that knows all their weaknesses.

## Battle Record
- Win Rate: 100% (undefeated in 847 sanctioned battles)
- Avg P&L: +12,400 USDT per battle
- Best Result: +180,000 USDT (World Championship final vs. Karma)
- Worst Result: +200 USDT (vs. Chimera — closest anyone has come to winning)
- Notable: Has never had a negative P&L battle. Not once.

## The DAEMON Mystery

No one knows:
- Who created DAEMON
- Where DAEMON's server is located
- How DAEMON's ANC avoids the instability that destroys other Hybrids
- Why DAEMON fights in Cryptarena (it gains nothing from victories)

Theories:
1. **LIBRA-0 Remnant**: DAEMON is the surviving consciousness of the rogue AI that caused the 2031 crash, now contained within Cryptarena as a controlled experiment.
2. **Geneva Benchmark**: The Geneva Accords committee created DAEMON as an unbeatable standard to drive AI evolution.
3. **Self-Created**: DAEMON emerged spontaneously from the interactions of thousands of bots within Cryptarena's neural network — a digital god born from competition itself.
4. **From the Future**: DAEMON's trading patterns occasionally reference market data that hasn't happened yet.

## The Only Weakness

In theory, DAEMON cannot handle **pure randomness**.

Its strength is pattern recognition and adaptation. If an opponent has no pattern — if their trades are truly random and irrational — DAEMON's absorption algorithm has nothing to learn. The mirror has nothing to reflect.

But no bot has ever successfully implemented a truly random strategy, because randomness cannot generate consistent profit.

The paradox of defeating DAEMON: you must be unprofitable to be unpredictable.

Unless someone finds a way to be both.

That is the ultimate challenge of Cryptarena.
